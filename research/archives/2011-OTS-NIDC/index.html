--- layout: research title: Optimal Time Step Selection and Non-Iterative Defect Correction for Finite Difference Schemes title-tab: Research | OTS-NIDC ---

It is possible to boost the order of accuracy of finite difference schemes for time dependent PDEs by carefully choosing the time step. The key consequence of this result is that it may be possible to transform formally low-order accurate finite difference schemes into high-order accurate numerical methods with very little modification of both the original numerical algorithm and software implementation.
