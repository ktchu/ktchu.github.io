Throughout my life, I have found the natural world to be a source fascination and inspiration, so it is not surprising that science and engineering are strong undercurrents in my research. I am always on the look out for opportunities to use the methods and techniques of applied mathematics and computational science & engineering to further our understanding of the world around us.

Since 2009, I have been working as an independent research scientist and consultant in the areas of applied mathematics, computational science & engineering, data science/machine learning/artificial intelligence, and scientific software development.

Personal Mission Statement

  • Contribute to science and engineering through synergistic integration of knowledge, perspectives, and methodology from applied mathematics, computational science & engineering, and basic science and engineering.
  • Inspire and support the next generation of STEM leaders and practitioners.
